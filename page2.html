WATCH: Southern University Dancing Dolls Perfom - "We Are One"

The beautiful and sensational Southern Univeristy Dancing Dolls recently performed at a game VS. Texas Southern University. If you've never seen these talented young dolls perform, you have surely been missing out on wonderful performances! These ladies dance infront of hundreds of people to the sounds of the Southern University Human Jukebox. This week, The band perform their rendition of "We Are One" by Maze ft. Frankie Beverly. You can also check them out on YouTube performing to other classic hits such as "Can You Stand The Rain" and "Purple Rain." I have to applaud this group of dolls for always keeping it classy and elegant. Balancing the stress of being a college student and a performer is certaintly not easy, but the Dolls make it look effortless. The band and the dolls have never disappointed! I am glad to present these talented ladies the very first spot in our weekly "Shining Spotlight." Keep up the great work ladies!
